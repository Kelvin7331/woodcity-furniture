Our Story

Where Wood
Meets Soul

Founded in 2022 by master craftsman Kelvin Woodley, WOODCITY began as a single workshop with a simple belief: furniture should be made to last generations. Today, our team of 14 artisans continues that tradition — hand-selecting every plank, hand-shaping every joint, and hand-finishing every surface.

Sustainably sourced hardwoods — certified FSC
Over 200 hand-finishing steps per piece
Zero-waste production — offcuts become décor
Lifetime structural guarantee on all furniture

Craftsmanship

How We
Build Heirlooms

Every WOODCITY piece passes through four meticulous stages — each one a commitment to the furniture you'll pass down one day.

01

Wood Selection

We hand-select every board from FSC-certified forests, inspecting for grain, density, and character. Only the top 15% of logs make the cut.

02

Kiln Drying

Lumber is slowly kiln-dried to 6–8% moisture content over 4–6 weeks, ensuring decades of dimensional stability.

03

Hand Joinery

Traditional mortise-and-tenon, dovetail, and box joints are cut by hand — no staples, no screws, no compromises.

04

Hand Finishing

Up to 12 coats of hand-rubbed oil finish, sanded to 400 grit between each layer for a surface that only improves with age.
